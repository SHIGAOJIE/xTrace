Blocklist

Blocklisted scripts are not allowed to load from source locations when they are referenced on a web page.

You can change what is in the lists here. For example, to blocklist http://www.baidu.com, you enter

   baidu.com

which also blocklists baidu.com, maps.baidu.com, hi.baidu.com, etc...
